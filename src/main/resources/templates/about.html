About the project

A travel planner built to feel inspiring, intelligent, and portfolio-worthy.

This platform combines AI-powered planning, destination visuals, responsive UI, and practical budgeting in MUR to create a richer travel planning experience.

Start Planning
Project overview

More than a simple CRUD app

AI Travel Planner is designed to feel like a premium digital travel assistant rather than a plain academic web app. It helps users create trips, generate AI-powered itineraries, visualize destinations, estimate budgets in MUR, and manage travel plans through a modern responsive dashboard.

The experience is focused on travel enthusiasts, demo-ready presentation, and clear portfolio value: authentication, SQLite persistence, AI text generation, image support, and a strong luxury-travel visual identity.

Authentication

Users can register, log in, save trip plans, and manage their own travel history securely.

Trip management

Create, edit, delete, and revisit trips with structured destination details and travel preferences.

AI planner

Generate itinerary sections, travel insights, packing lists, and checklist items based on trip inputs.

Destination visuals

Use direct image URLs or OpenAI-generated visuals to make trips feel more immersive and aspirational.

How it works

The user flow

1
Create an account

Register or log in to access your private travel planner workspace.

2
Create a trip

Enter destination, dates, travel style, number of travellers, interests, and budget in MUR.

3
Generate AI content

The planner creates itinerary sections, insights, checklist items, and optional destination visuals.

4
Manage and refine

Return later to edit the trip, regenerate content, and maintain a polished travel history.
